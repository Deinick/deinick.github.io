"ARTIST ME"

Drawing

        Drawing has always been my passion.
Ever since I was a child, I’ve had a natural
talent for translating the world around me
onto paper or canvas.

        Drawing, in particular, has been more
than just a skill — it’s a form of
self-expression that fuels my creativity no
matter where I am. As an experienced artist,
I’ve learned to observe the world from
multiple perspectives, which allows me to
generate fresh ideas and bring unique visual
concepts to life in every project I take on.
Art isn't just something I do — it’s a way of
thinking, seeing, and communicating.

Video filming, photography, and blogging

Video filming, photography, and blogging are the main directions where I develop and
 dedicate most of my free time.
  Capturing moments and visual storytelling have become an integral part of my identity.
Over time, picturing scenes, emotions, and atmospheres turned from a hobby into a
creative practice I deeply value. Being confident in front of the camera and having a
strong sense of performance has always been one of my natural strengths — allowing me
not only to create but to connect with audiences through expression, presence, and
emotion.
I have solid skills in editing and have worked with:
- Adobe Premiere Pro
- Adobe After Effects
- DaVinci Resolve
- Sony Vegas Pro 19
- Adobe Photoshop
These tools have become a regular part of my creative work.

Piano

Playing the piano has always been one of my favorite hobbies. After graduating from music school at the age of 14, with over seven years of piano training behind me, I began to explore other forms of art — and I quickly realized how deeply my musical background influenced my growth in those areas.


The discipline, quick thinking, and adaptability developed through mastering complex piano pieces have translated into valuable soft skills. These qualities have helped me thrive not only in creative fields like acting, but also in physically demanding disciplines such as martial arts (karate). Music taught me how to stay composed under pressure, how to persist through challenge, and how to express myself — lessons that continue to shape my personal and artistic journey.
